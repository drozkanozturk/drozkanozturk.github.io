About

I am a Full Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Giresun University. I earned my M.S. in Applied Mathematics, Ph.D. in Mathematics, and a second M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Missouri University of Science and Technology.

My research focuses on differential and difference equations and time scales theory, with applications in biology and engineering — from modeling infectious diseases such as monkeypox, HIV, tuberculosis, and HPV, to developing control strategies for unmanned aerial and ground vehicles.
